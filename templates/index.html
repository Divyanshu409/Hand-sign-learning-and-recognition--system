How It Works
  1. Press Start — your camera activates
  2. Show any hand sign (A–Z or special)
  3. The gold arc fills as you hold steady
  4. At 1 second → sign is appended automatically
  5. Change sign before repeating the same letter
  6. Use SPACE / DEL / SPEAK / COPY signs or the buttons

About the Project

Mission, technology, and purpose

The Hand Sign Learning and Recognition System is designed to foster connections where spoken words are limited, offering a channel for genuine expression and understanding. This system empowers individuals who are exploring the nuances of hand-based communication, enabling them to grasp subtle gestures and their intended meanings through an interactive, AI-powered interface.
